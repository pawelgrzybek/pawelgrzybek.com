I published [my list of defaults last time in 2023](/my-defaults-2023/), but a few things have changed since then. Inspired by [Bud Spencer's comment](/from-1password-to-apple-passwords/#comment-3a11c014), here it is, my list of defaults as of 2026. Not a drastic change, but to make it a little easier to parse, I annotated the new things. If you are curious about the hardware, like a good IndieWeb citizen, [I have a /uses page](/uses), so have a peek there.

- **Mail Client**: Apple Mail
- **Mail Server**: [Fastmail](https://www.fastmail.com)
- **Notes**: Apple Notes
- **To-Do**: Apple Reminders
- **Photo Shooting**: [Sony α7R III](https://www.sony.co.uk/electronics/interchangeable-lens-cameras/ilce-7rm3) and the default Camera app on iOS (updated)
- **Photo Management**: [Photomator](https://www.pixelmator.com/photomator/) (updated)
- **Calendar**: Apple Calendar
- **Cloud File Storage**: iCloud Drive (Apple)
- **RSS**: [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com/) (client), iCloud (server) (updated)
- **Contacts**: Apple Contacts
- **Browser**: Safari
- **Chat**: Apple Messages
- **Bookmarks**: Safari (Bookmarks) (freakin' hate this thing, but I will share some update on this one soon)
- **Read It Later**: Apple Reminders (updated)
- **Word Processing**: Apple Pages
- **Spreadsheets**: Apple Numbers
- **Presentations**: Apple Keynote
- **Shopping Lists**: Apple Reminders
- **Meal Planning**: NA
- **Budgeting and Personal Finance**: Apple Numbers
- **News**: [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com/) (client), iCloud (server) (updated)
- **Music**: Apple Music
- **Podcasts**: Apple Podcasts (updated)
- **Password Management**: Apple Passwords (updated)

Some of the other extras I use frequently.

- **Code Editor**: [Neovim](https://neovim.io/) (updated)
- **Mastodon**: [Ivory](https://tapbots.com/ivory/)
- **Terminal**: [Ghostty](https://ghostty.org/) (updated)
- **Launcher**: [Raycast](https://www.raycast.com)
